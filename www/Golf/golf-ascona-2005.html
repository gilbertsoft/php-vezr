We are happy to invite you to the Interbourse Golf 2005 hosted by the Swiss team. It will take place from May 29th to June 4th 2005 in Ascona, in the southern part of Switzerland. Ascona is a charming village just at the lakeside, surrounded by the mountains. The airport of Milan (Italy) is only a one and a half hour drive away. There are three superior golf courses around Ascona and many more in the north of Italy. We will stay at the famous EdenRoc Hotel***** known for its two exquisite Restaurants with a fantastic view over the Lago Maggiore.
