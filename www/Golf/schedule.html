Welcome to Interbourse Golf Ascona 2005 Switzerland

  
     
 
 
The Schedule:
  
               
 
Sunday May 29th
  
  Arrival at the EdenRoc Hotel***** in Ascona, Switzerland. Registration and give-aways, introduction of the hotel facilities.We are delighted to kick off this first Interbourse Golf week in Switzerland with the Welcome Drinks Party right next to the private harbour of the EdenRoc Hotel (edenroc.ch) in Ascona. The magic view over the Lago Maggiore and the unique atmosphere in the hotel park are outstanding.  
   
Sponsor: SWX Swiss Exchange
               
 
Monday May 30th
  
This 1st day of the Nations Cup starts with an unforgettable journey over the Lago Maggiore by boat. Thereafter we will bring you by bus to one of the oldest golf clubs of Italy, the Golf Club Varese (golfclubvarese.it), where the 1st round of the Nations Cup will be played. For the nongolfers we provide during the golf tournament a journey to Varese for shopping and sightseeing. Late afternoon we will all come together and enjoy an early Italian speciality dinner at the venue of the Golf Club Varese, an old and ancient cloister and monastery, overlooking the lake of Varese. We shall return to Ascona later on by coach.  
         
  Sponsor: UBS Wealth Management  
   
 
Tuesday May 31st
  
  The 2nd round of the Nations Cup will be played at the Golf Club Patriziale Ascona (golf.ascona.ch). Beautifully placed between old pine trees and flowers and just 10 minutes walk away from the EdenRoc Hotel, there will be two shuttle buses to bring you there in time for your tee time. In the early evening we will then start a typical Swiss Ticino Party with cocktails and dinner at the Golf Club Patriziale Ascona with many local specialities.  
         
  Sponsor: UBS Wealth Management  
               
 
Wednesday June 1st
  
  This day is fully at your disposal. We can suggest to you shopping trips to Milan (approx. 1 ½ hour drive away) or Lugano ( 40 min. drive away) or some sightseeing in the region. For the serious golfers we will, upon request, reserve tee times on two championships courses in Italy. They both are about 1 ½ hours drive away from Ascona, but worthwhile a trip, Circolo Golf Bogogno (circologolfbogogno.com) and Golf Club Castelconturbia. For further information please contact either the EdenRoc Hotel or the organising committee.  
   
 
Thursday June 2nd
  
  We will play a four-ball format at the Golf Guerre Losone (www.golflosone.ch), which is only 10 minutes drive away from our hotel. Again we will provide two shuttle buses to bring you there for your tee time. The Kalcutta Auction and Coctail will be held at the EdenRoc Hotel.  
         
  Sponsor: Bank Leu AG  
      Bernhard L. Madoff Investment Securities
(Bernhard L. & Peter B. Madoff)		  
       
 
Friday June 3rd
  
  The final day of this Interbourse week starts with the Kalcutta Golf Tournament, which will be held again at the Golf Club Patriziale Ascona. The usual transportation will be organised for your convenience. The Gala Dinner will be held at the EdenRoc Hotel. Various entertainments will complete a wonderful week of Interbourse Golf in Switzerland.  
         
Sponsors: Frankfurter Golf Friends
Frédéric Keller Consulting
AFS Group Brokers
 
         
               
 
Saturday June 4th
  
 
Departure
