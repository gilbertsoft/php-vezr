Varese

Tuesday May 31st
  
               
 
Ascona

Thursday June 2nd
  
               
 

Brissago Island
10.30 a.m. meeting at the hotel lobby with the guide
A refreshing boat ride brings you to the botanical gardens of the Canton Ticino, created by the Russian
Baroness Antoinette de St. Leger in the year 1885. Beside beautiful flowers, the garden is meant to
show: rare, special and useful plants as local display for didactic purpose. All together, there are more
than 1500 species from all over the world. On the island there is a villa with restaurant where you will
have your lunch (not included in the price).
02.00 a.m. arrival in Ascona

           
 
Friday June 3rd
  
               
Lugano
10.30 a.m. meeting at the hotel lobby with the guide
Transfer by bus to Lugano, guided visiting tour and lunch at the well-known restaurant
Grand Caffé al Porto (not included in the price). After lunch free time and around for shopping
and sightseeing.
03.00 p.m. return to Ascona.
               
 
Please note that the Program on Wednesday is no included
